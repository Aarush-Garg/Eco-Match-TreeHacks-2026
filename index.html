About Eco-Match

Our Mission

We connect talented professionals with meaningful careers in climate technology. Our platform aggregates over 1000 opportunities across 80+ climate subsectors‚Äîranging from ocean carbon capture to insect-based alternative proteins and fats‚Äîleveraging data from leading climate-tech companies and the Climate Tech Map framework.

Founders

Eco-Match was founded by Martin, Grant, and Aarush ‚Äî a team of entrepreneurs from Stanford who experienced firsthand the challenge of searching for climate-tech jobs. Without a structured way to explore the space, navigating the landscape of climate careers felt overwhelming and fragmented. That frustration became our inspiration: we built Eco-Match to give students searching for internships, professionals looking to pivot into sustainability, and anyone passionate about climate action the clarity and tools we wished we had.

Why Climate Tech Matters

The climate crisis is the defining challenge of our generation. Climate tech companies are building the solutions we need to decarbonize our economy - from renewable energy and electric vehicles to carbon removal and sustainable agriculture. These aren't just jobs; they're opportunities to work on humanity's most important challenge.

How It Works

Our AI-powered career counselor uses Google Gemini to match your skills, experience, and interests with relevant climate-tech opportunities. We track 41 key skills, 17 work areas, and opportunities across all experience levels - from internships to executive positions.
