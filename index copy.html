About Climate-Tech Career Hub

Our Mission

We connect talented professionals with meaningful careers in climate technology. Our platform aggregates opportunities across the 6 key climate sectors identified by the Climate Tech Map framework, created by Breakthrough Energy, Stanford, and McKinsey.

Why Climate Tech Matters

The climate crisis is the defining challenge of our generation. Climate tech companies are building the solutions we need to decarbonize our economy - from renewable energy and electric vehicles to carbon removal and sustainable agriculture. These aren't just jobs; they're opportunities to work on humanity's most important challenge.

How It Works

Our AI-powered career counselor uses Google Gemini to match your skills, experience, and interests with relevant climate-tech opportunities. We track 41 key skills, 17 work areas, and opportunities across all experience levels - from internships to executive positions.
